What is DuoDirect?

DuoDirect is an innovative meeting management platform for IT companies. It is specially designed to facilitate the meeting planning process. Although the platform does not hold meetings itself, it ensures efficient meeting organization and facilitates collaboration before and after meetings.

The main advantages of the product

They will make your planning more convenient

Smart scheduling

DuoDirect offers an intelligent scheduling system that helps you determine the best time to meet.

Clear agenda

DuoDirect allows meeting organizers to set a clear agenda and describe the purpose of the meeting.

Invite and confirm participation

DuoDirect provides the ability to invite participants to a meeting and receive their confirmation of attendance.

Calendar reminders and support

DuoDirect sends meeting reminders to all participants to ensure they are prepared in a timely manner.
